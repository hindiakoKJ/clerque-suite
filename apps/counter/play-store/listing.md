## Full description (max 4000 chars)

```
Clerque Counter is the front-of-house companion to Clerque Cloud — the
all-in-one POS + accounting platform built for Filipino small businesses.

Install on an Android tablet at the counter. Sign in once with your
Clerque account. That's it. No in-app purchase, no separate billing —
your existing Clerque subscription at clerque.com powers the whole thing.

★ BUILT FOR PH SMBs ★

— Pre-loaded for Philippine business types: coffee shops & restaurants,
  laundry shops, pharmacies, retail / sari-sari stores
— BIR-compliant receipt printing (gap-free OR sequence, Non-VAT / VAT
  split, "Pang-opisyal na Resibo" bilingual closing)
— Senior Citizen / PWD discount with RA 9994 attestation built in
— GCash + PayMaya as first-class payment methods (never hidden under
  "Other") with brand-correct QR display on the customer screen

★ FAST AT THE TILL ★

— 64dp tap targets for one-handed cashier use
— Long-press = void (supervisor PIN required)
— Swipe-left = remove (no auth, before order finalizes)
— Modifier groups in a native bottom sheet — required radios,
  optional checkboxes, live price impact
— Filipino-aware labels: Bayad, Sukli, Bayaran, Pang-opisyal na Resibo

★ WORKS OFFLINE ★

— Sales survive WiFi blips — local SQLite outbox queues every sale
— Drains to Cloud automatically when connectivity returns
— Amber "Working offline" banner (warning, not error — offline is
  normal at the PH cashier counter)
— Z-read still reconciles correctly across offline shifts

★ MULTI-DEVICE WITHOUT MULTI-LOGIN ★

— Pair a customer-facing TV with a 4-digit code from Settings →
  Displays. No second account, no second app install.
— Same flow for Kitchen / Bar displays (KDS)
— Each device just polls — no overhead

★ COMPLIANCE & SAFETY ★

— RA 9994 Senior / PWD attestation (ID, owner name, 20% off + VAT exempt)
— RA 9165 §61 controlled-substance gate for pharmacies (supervisor PRC
  license required to add Schedule II drugs)
— RA 9211 18+ banner for regulated retail SKUs (cigarettes, alcohol)
— BIR Z-read with full Non-VAT / VAT breakdown, void ledger, cash
  variance pill

★ SOLO TIER LINEUP (sold separately at clerque.com) ★

— Solo Lite ₱199/mo  · 1 user  · 5 recipe products
— Solo Standard ₱399/mo · 3 users · unlimited recipes + 10 FEFO items
                         · customer phone-lookup · receipt header/footer
— Solo Pro ₱499/mo  · 5 users  · audit log + advanced reports
                         · Loyalty Pro stamps + auto-backup + API

Subscriptions sold separately at clerque.com — Counter never sells
anything in-app (consumption-only app pattern).

★ HARDWARE ★

Recommended: Samsung Galaxy Tab A8 10.5″ landscape, any Bluetooth
ESC/POS thermal printer (BIR-accredited), any USB-OTG keyboard-wedge
barcode scanner. Camera barcode scanning also supported as a fallback.

Need help? Visit help.clerque.com or email support@clerque.com.
```

Free download. In-app purchases: **none**. The app is a sign-in client
for an existing Clerque subscription sold at clerque.com.

> 🚨 Important for Play Console review: Clerque Counter qualifies for
> the "consumption-only app" exemption from Google Play Billing because
> it does not sell digital goods in-app. The Clerque subscription is a
> SaaS service (not in-app digital content) sold separately for use
> across web + this Android client. Same model as Spotify, Netflix,
> Audible. If asked: cite the **Play Console policy "Consumption-only
> apps" exception under Payments**.
